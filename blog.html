1. Med Device Online

Med Device Online is a resource for medical device professionals involved in the early phases of medical device and diagnostics development. Med Device Online provides insights and updates on design and development, regulations and compliance, manufacturing, and supply chain in the medical device industry. Med Device Online’s goal is to keep industry professionals and investors well-informed of the latest technological advances and trends in Medical Technology (MedTech), as well as provide professional guidance. Med Device Online provides a free newsletter and e-books. Med Device Online is part of a network of websites where life science companies may submit and pay for the publication of their articles, as well as run content marketing campaigns.

Listed below are the 3 top articles of the Med Device Online.

  • An Overview of the 5 Types Of Neuromodulation Devices
  • What Does EN ISO 14971:2019’s New Amendment Mean For Harmonization?
  • Medical Device QMS Cybersecurity: Threat Modeling

2. MedTech Intelligence

MedTech Intelligence is a professional community for medical technology professionals focused on improving patient outcomes, increasing access to care, and reducing healthcare costs. MedTech Intelligence is a source of information on digital health, economics, manufacturing execution, product design and development, quality, and regulatory affairs in the medical technology industry. MedTech Intelligence provides information to medical technology innovators, medical device and diagnostics manufacturers, digital health and patient care providers, and industry regulators. MedTech Intelligence provides free educational resources, such as a library, online events, and webinars. MedTech Intelligence offers paid advertising opportunities to MedTech companies.

Listed below are the 3 top articles of the MedTech Intelligence.

  • State-of-the-Art: Unpacking MDR’s Evolving Requirements
  • Reduce Your Medical Device Time-to-Market with CPQ Software
  • From Concept to Market: The Strategic Journey from IND to NDA

3. Medical Device and Diagnostic Industry (MD+DI)

MD+DI is a resource for manufacturers of medical devices and in vitro diagnostic products conducting device design and development. MD+DI provides comprehensive topical coverage on product development, manufacturing, regulatory and quality, digital health, and business in the medical device industry. MD+DI offers information to medical technology industry professionals. MD+DI also offers free webinars, online events, and white papers. MD+DI provides a channel for medical device companies to reach active buyers through paid advertisements.

Listed below are the 3 top articles of the MD+DI.

  • Top 40 Medical Device Companies
  • Your Smartwatch Can’t Measure Blood Glucose Levels… Yet
  • ‘Plan A’ Offers a New Option in Male Birth Control
